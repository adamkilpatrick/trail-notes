
Welcome to my webzone. If you are here you are hopefully interested in looking at details about whether or not I'm alive or where I am on the Appalachian Trail because that is mostly what this is. Enough people had asked me to send them updates/details about it and sending individual updates to a ton of people or writing a full on blog or posting a bunch of shit to Instagram all sound awful so I settled on this. I had planned on writing as much down as I could for this [because David Lynch says to](https://youtu.be/7vVIHLURsr4?si=z5NQgiiB4Xeac-Rv). So this is going to be (mostly) everything I'm writing down, published to the open Internet ([[Misc/Tech.md | how this works]]). There's no comments section, no algorithm feeds, no interactivity at all. This is purely a static collection of ramblings, notes, pictures, and maybe some data if I can commit to being consistent with it.
